Graduate Management Trainee, Solution Tech Limited

Solution Tech Limited is a technology company that provides innovative solutions to various industries. Our focus is on developing and implementing cutting-edge technology to help businesses and organizations improve their operations, increase efficiency, and reduce costs. Our areas of expertise are:

  • Software development and integration
  • IT consulting and management
  • Cybersecurity solutions
  • Data analytics and science
  • Artificial intelligence and machine learning
  • Cloud computing and infrastructure
  • Training with Aptech Computer Education franchise

The company serves a range of industries, such as:

  • Finance and banking
  • Healthcare and medical
  • Retail and e-commerce
  • Manufacturing and logistics
  • Government and public sector

We are searching for candidates who are eager to acquire new skills and unleash their career potential with our Graduate Management Trainee program. Take the first step towards a being a future leader in our company and in the industry.

What to expect
  • Participation in Sales & Marketing, Technology and Customer Service efforts within the company
  • Briefing about the company's business strategy, followed by active engagement in adjusting this strategy
  • Active mentoring to determine and cultivate your unique talents
  • Clear pathway to securing a permanent role within the company after 6 months
Requirements
  • University graduates of any field of study with at least a Second Class Lower (2:2)
  • Age limit is 30 years and below
  • Must have completed N.Y.S.C.

This advert has minimum requirements listed. Management reserves the right to use additional relevant information as criteria for shortlisting. Work locations are onsite at Uyo and Lagos.

Solution Tech Limited respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it in accordance with applicable laws and using generally-accepted industry practices. If you have any questions or concerns about Solution Tech's collection and use of your personal information, please contact us at contact@aptech-uyo.info.
